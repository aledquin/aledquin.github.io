A little about me

I've tried making my personal webpage since I was 15. Every time I started I had so many ideas, and every time I felt disappointed and scared of showing myself. So I pushed myself year after year to begin again — and it was the same for dancing, singing, sports, arts, crafts, painting, cooking, cocktails, programming, AI, writing, fixing my traumas, feeling… sooo many things.

Do I rest? I feel a little guilty because it's very hard for me to feel comfortable doing nothing. In my mind it is how it is, but I'm trying to share a little of myself here, as a therapeutic complement. Right now I'm even learning new words — I should share that too!

If you are here and you are interested in the things I will share, thank you, and I hope you enjoy.

I'm Alvaro Quintana Carvacho, originally from Chile. I love building things — whether that's a well-balanced Pisco Sour, a new coding tool, or a guacamole sauce that fits perfectly in a bottle. This site is my corner of the internet where all those things live together.
